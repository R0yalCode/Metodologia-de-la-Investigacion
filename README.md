# Analysis of the Use of Autoencoders in Dimensionality Reduction

## Research Project Repository

This repository documents the complete research workflow followed to answer the research question:

**"What is the role and effectiveness of Autoencoders in dimensionality reduction?"**

The objective of this repository is to provide full methodological transparency and reproducibility of the literature review process conducted during the course *Research Methodology in Computing*.

## Research Workflow

The study followed the methodology proposed by the course instructor:

1. Definition of the research question.
2. Keyword extraction and search-string construction.
3. Execution of searches in IEEE Xplore and ACM Digital Library.
4. Expansion to ScienceDirect, Springer and Lens.
5. Management of references using Mendeley.
6. Selection and prioritization of scientific articles.
7. Data extraction using NotebookLM.
8. Scopus metadata analysis.
9. Scientific writing using Overleaf and LaTeX.
10. Final synthesis of evidence.

## Repository Contents

The repository includes:

* Search strategies.
* Database screenshots.
* Search strings.
* Exported metadata.
* BibTeX files.
* Mendeley evidence.
* NotebookLM prompts.
* Extraction matrices.
* Scopus AI reports.
* LaTeX source files.
* Final research paper.

## Reproducibility

All files included in this repository allow any researcher to replicate the complete review process and verify each methodological decision made during the development of the project.
